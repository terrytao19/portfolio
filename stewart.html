Stewart Platform

CAD for my stewart platform, in Solidworks.
I controlled the 6 hobby servos with an arduino mounted to the end effector.
I used an IMU and PID controllers to dampen the acceleration of the end effector in all 3 translational directions and yaw, and I used the angle estimate to keep the end effector level in roll and pitch
